Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

What types of textile waste do you accept?

We accept a wide range of textile byproducts including cotton, polyester, nylon, blends, cutting scraps, mill ends, and post-industrial textile waste. Contact us with details about your waste stream and we'll let you know how we can help.

What areas do you service for collection?

We primarily serve manufacturers in the Northeast United States, with our base of operations in New York. For larger volumes, we can arrange collection and transportation from a wider geographic area.

Can you provide samples before I order?

Absolutely. We encourage potential customers to request samples of our processed fibers. This ensures our products meet your specifications before you commit to a purchase.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum quantities vary by fiber type and grade. Contact us with your requirements and we'll work with you to find a solution that fits your needs, whether you're looking for a trial quantity or ongoing supply.
