Our Heritage

Rooted in New York. Built on Hard Work.

Fiber Conversion Inc. was founded in 1909 in the heart of New York's industrial corridor. In an era when textile waste was simply discarded, our founders saw an opportunity: transforming what others threw away into materials that manufacturers needed.

Over the decades, we've evolved our processes and expanded our capabilities, but our core principle has never changed — every fiber has value, and nothing should go to waste. That philosophy has sustained our business through two world wars, multiple recessions, and the complete transformation of the American textile industry.

Today, as sustainability becomes a global priority, the work we've been doing for over a century has never been more relevant. We continue to serve the New York community and manufacturers throughout the Northeast with the same dedication to quality and service that defined our founding.

Our Journey

Milestones Across the Decades

From our founding to the present day, a look at the moments that shaped who we are.

1909

Founded in New York

Fiber Conversion Inc. opens its doors as a small textile reclamation operation, serving local garment manufacturers and mills.

1940s

Wartime Contribution

During World War II, the company plays a vital role in fiber recovery efforts, recycling materials to support the war-time economy and reduce waste during resource scarcity.

1960s

Expanded Operations

Growing demand for recycled fibers leads to expanded processing capacity and new partnerships with manufacturers throughout the Northeast.

1980s

Modernized Processing

Investment in modern fiber processing equipment allows us to handle a broader range of textile waste and produce higher-quality recycled fibers.

2000s

Sustainability Leadership

As environmental awareness grows, Fiber Conversion becomes a recognized leader in closed-loop textile recycling, partnering with companies committed to reducing their environmental impact.

Today

Continuing the Legacy

With over 115 years of operation, we continue to innovate our processes while staying true to the principles that have guided us since day one: quality, service, and sustainability.
